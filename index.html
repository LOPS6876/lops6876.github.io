Buyer questions

Answers before the first call.

The first conversation should be productive. These questions help buyers prepare clear product, packaging and shipment requirements.

What products does SOVIRA GLOBAL export?

SOVIRA GLOBAL focuses on agricultural commodities, processed food ingredients, sustainable bagasse packaging, private label manufacturing, OEM manufacturing, and customized sourcing solutions from India.

Can you support private label packaging?

Yes. We can coordinate private label packaging for selected products, including artwork, pack format, carton planning, and sample-led approval, subject to product and supplier confirmation.

Do you provide samples?

Samples can be coordinated where applicable. Sample availability, courier cost, and timeline depend on product, destination, and buyer requirements.

What is the MOQ?

MOQ is confirmed based on product, grade, packaging format, destination, and supplier conditions. Product pages use indicative or available-on-request placeholders until final confirmation.

Which countries can you export to?

We support export requirements for buyers across major global regions, including the Middle East, Europe, North America, Africa, Southeast Asia, and Oceania, subject to product and destination requirements.

Can you help with documentation?

Yes. Documentation support may include commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, lab reports, phytosanitary certificate where applicable, and other destination-specific documents on request.

How quickly do you respond?

We aim to respond to qualified inquiries within 24 business hours.

How do I request a quote?

Use the inquiry form, WhatsApp export desk, or email soviraglobal@gmail.com with product, destination, quantity, packaging requirement, and target timeline.
