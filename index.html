Yes. Private label support is available for selected products, pack sizes and buyer requirements. Final options depend on product format, quantity and packaging feasibility.

Yes. Samples can be coordinated where applicable. Sample availability, courier cost and timeline depend on product, destination and buyer requirements.

What products do you export?

Yes. We can coordinate retail pouches, bulk formats, private label packs and export cartons based on buyer requirements.
