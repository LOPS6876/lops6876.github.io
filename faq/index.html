FAQ

Frequently asked questions about SOVIRA GLOBAL products, MOQ, samples, private label, packaging, quality, documentation, shipping, payments and sustainability.

Company

Company questions

What is SOVIRA GLOBAL?

SOVIRA GLOBAL is an India-based export and import company focused on agricultural commodities, processed food ingredients, sustainable packaging, private label, OEM and custom sourcing solutions.

Where is SOVIRA GLOBAL based?

SOVIRA GLOBAL is based in India and supports international buyers across global destination markets subject to product and documentation requirements.

Products

Products questions

Which products are available?

The current catalogue includes makhana, dehydrated onion and garlic, turmeric, jaggery, tea premix, millets, bagasse plates, bagasse bowls, sugarcane bagasse raw material and sourcing solutions.

Can you source products beyond the listed catalogue?

Yes. Export sourcing and international procurement support can be explored for buyer-specific categories in India.

Private Label

Private Label questions

Can you support private label programs?

Yes, private label can be explored for selected products with packaging, artwork, label and sample coordination.

Can you help with artwork?

Artwork dimensions, label placement and print-ready placeholders can be coordinated, while final compliance approval should come from the buyer or destination-market advisor.

MOQ & Samples

MOQ & Samples questions

What is the MOQ?

MOQ depends on product, grade, packaging, private label requirement, destination and supplier conditions.

Can I request a sample?

Samples can be coordinated where applicable after product, grade, packaging and destination details are reviewed.

Packaging

Packaging questions

What packaging formats are supported?

Retail pouches, bulk bags, cartons, foodservice packs, private label packs and custom cartons can be explored by product.

Can you support multilingual labels?

Multilingual labels can be planned as a placeholder requirement, subject to buyer-side destination compliance review.

Quality

Quality questions

What quality checks are available?

Supplier screening, batch inspection support, packaging inspection, lab test support and pre-shipment inspection can be explored depending on product and buyer requirement.

Do you guarantee certifications?

No unverified certification claims are made. Certification and documentation status must be confirmed for each product and supplier.

Documentation

Documentation questions

What documents can you support?

Commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, lab reports, phytosanitary certificate where applicable, fumigation certificate where applicable and buyer-requested documents can be discussed.

Who confirms HS codes?

HS codes should be confirmed based on product form, grade and destination-country classification.

Shipping

Shipping questions

Which shipment modes are available?

Shipment planning depends on product, quantity, destination, Incoterms and packaging. Sea and courier sample movements can be discussed where applicable.

Can you calculate container capacity?

Container capacity is available on request after final pack, carton dimensions, net weight, gross weight and palletization are confirmed.

Payments

Payments questions

What payment terms are available?

Payment terms are confirmed after buyer due diligence, product selection, supplier terms and commercial agreement.

Do you publish prices online?

No. Export pricing depends on product grade, quantity, packaging, destination, freight assumptions and market conditions.

Sustainability

Sustainability questions

Do you offer sustainable packaging?

Yes. Bagasse plates, bagasse bowls and sugarcane bagasse raw material are part of the sustainable packaging catalogue.

Are sustainability certificates available?

Documentation can be shared where available and applicable. The website avoids unverified sustainability claims.
