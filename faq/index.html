Buyer FAQ

Practical answers for global buyers.

Quick answers about product portfolio, private label, MOQ, packaging, samples and inquiry process.

Do you offer private label packaging?

Yes. Private label support is available for selected products, pack sizes and buyer requirements. Final options depend on product format, quantity and packaging feasibility.

Can I request samples?

Yes. Samples can be coordinated where applicable. Sample availability, courier cost and timeline depend on product, destination and buyer requirements.

What products do you export?

SOVIRA GLOBAL focuses on jaggery powder, jaggery cubes, premium makhana, flavoured makhana, tea premix, dehydrated onion flakes, dehydrated garlic flakes and sugarcane bagasse products.

Do you support custom packaging?

Yes. We can coordinate retail pouches, bulk formats, private label packs and export cartons based on buyer requirements.

What is the MOQ?

MOQ is confirmed based on product, grade, pack format, destination and supplier conditions. Use the inquiry form to request a product-specific MOQ.

Do you handle bulk supply?

Bulk supply support is available across suitable product categories after requirement review, sample confirmation and packaging finalization.
