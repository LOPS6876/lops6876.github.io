Frequently asked questions

Customized Packaging procurement questions.

Can SOVIRA GLOBAL export Customized Packaging?

Yes. SOVIRA GLOBAL can support export inquiries for Customized Packaging, subject to product availability, buyer specification, packaging, destination requirements, and final commercial confirmation.

What is the MOQ for Customized Packaging?

MOQ is available on request and depends on product grade, pack format, destination, supplier terms, and whether private label or custom packaging is required.

Can I request samples before placing an order?

Samples can be coordinated where applicable. Sample availability, courier cost, and timing are confirmed after reviewing the buyer requirement.

Do you provide private label packaging?

Private label packaging can be explored for selected products, including artwork, pack size, carton planning, and label coordination placeholders.

What documents can be provided?

Common documents may include commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, lab reports, phytosanitary certificate where applicable, and other documents requested by the buyer or destination market.
