How trade moves

Export Process

A premium interactive export process for SOVIRA GLOBAL buyers, covering inquiry, requirement analysis, quotation, samples, production, inspection, packaging, documentation, shipping, delivery and after-sales support.

Start Inquiry

Inquiry

Share product, destination, packaging, and quantity requirements.

Requirement Analysis

We clarify specifications, compliance expectations, shipment route, and buyer priorities.

Quotation

You receive a structured quote with product, packaging, and shipment assumptions clearly stated.

Sample Approval

Samples are coordinated where applicable before final production or procurement commitment.

Production

Supplier execution begins after approval of grade, pack format, artwork, and commercial terms.

Quality Inspection

Relevant quality and packaging checks are coordinated before dispatch.

Packaging

Products are packed for the selected channel, destination, and shipment plan.

Documentation

Export paperwork and buyer-requested documents are reviewed before shipment.

Shipping

Logistics coordination is aligned to the agreed Incoterm, destination port, and timeline.

Delivery

Shipment documentation and communication support continue through arrival milestones.

After Sales Support

Feedback, repeat orders, improvements, and future sourcing plans are handled systematically.
